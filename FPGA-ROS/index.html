FPGA ROS

FPGA-ROS is the work developed by the Chair of Adaptive Dynamic Systems focusing on integrating FPGAs into robotics. The FPGA Interfaces for Robotics Middlewares (FIRM) tool was developed for seamless integration. For more information, check out the repository below and the respective publications.
